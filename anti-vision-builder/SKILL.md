# Anti-Vision Builder

Explicitly articulate the life you refuse to live — creating negative motivation and an automatic decision filter.

## 5-Step Process

### Step 1: 10-Year Drift
"If you continue your exact current patterns for 10 years, where do you end up?" Push for specifics: body, relationships, finances, morning feelings. Then: "What's the worst *likely* outcome — not catastrophizing, but what you've seen happen to others on this path?"

### Step 2: Unbearable Circumstances
"What circumstances would you find absolutely unbearable?" Explore across: health, relationships, work, financial, identity.

### Step 3: The Waste Question
"What would make you feel, at the end, that you wasted your life?" Reveals core values in negative space — "wasted" implies something valuable to spend.

### Step 4: Hidden Tolerations
"What are you currently tolerating that you secretly despise?" Present tolerations predict future anti-vision: small compromises become large concessions, "temporary" becomes permanent.

### Step 5: Single-Sentence Condensation
Force ALL insights into ONE sentence: **"The life I refuse to live is: _______________"**

Constraint: must reference the user's own choices, not external circumstances. "I don't want bad luck" is not an anti-vision.

## Decision Filter

```
Any decision → "Does this move toward or away from my anti-vision?"
TOWARD: Reject immediately
AWAY: Consider further
NEUTRAL: Examine more closely
```
