# Life Reset Protocol

Guide through Dan Koe's 1-Day Life Reset Protocol — structured approach to radical life clarity through psychological excavation, autopilot interruption, and action synthesis.

## Core Principle

Most people fail at life change because they focus on actions (second-order) instead of identity (first-order), never explicitly define what they want/don't want, and run on autopilot. This protocol interrupts autopilot and forces clarity in a single day.

## The 1-Day Protocol

### Phase 1: Morning — Psychological Excavation (1-2 hours)

**Anti-Vision Questions** (what you refuse to accept):
1. "If I continue my current patterns for 10 years, where do I end up?"
2. "What does my worst realistic future look like?"
3. "What circumstances would I find absolutely unbearable?"
4. "What would make me feel I wasted my life?"
5. "What am I tolerating that I secretly despise?"

> For deep anti-vision work, see `anti-vision-builder` skill.

**Minimum Viable Vision Questions** (what you actually want):
1. "What would I want if no one else could see?"
2. "Strip away status markers — what remains?"
3. "What is the minimum I need to feel my life is well-lived?"
4. "What would I regret NOT doing?"
5. "If I had 5 years left, what would become urgent?"

**Output**: Raw, unfiltered answers. Don't edit. Don't judge.

### Phase 2: Mid-Day — Interrupting Autopilot

Set 3-5 phone reminders (10am, 1pm, 4pm, 7pm). At each: pause, ask **"Am I moving toward the life I hate or the life I want?"**, note the honest answer, make immediate micro-correction if toward anti-vision.

Observe: What activities move you toward anti-vision vs. vision? Where are you on autopilot?

### Phase 3: Evening — Synthesis & Action (1 hour)

1. **Condense Anti-Vision**: "The life I refuse to live is: _______________"
2. **Condense Vision**: "What I actually want is: _______________"
3. **One-Year Goal**: What single outcome would most advance you?
4. **Daily Levers**: 2-3 daily actions that directly move toward the one-year goal
5. **First Action**: Smallest action you can take RIGHT NOW

## Quick Facilitation

```
User indicates stuck/needing direction
        ↓

        ↓

QUICK: Phase 2 question only
        ↓
Always end with: "What's the smallest action you can take right now?"
```

## Key Rules

- Force single sentences over complex explanations — minimum viable over maximum ambition
- Don't accept vague vision statements ("be happy") or multiple competing visions — force ONE
- Don't skip the anti-vision — negative clarity is essential
- Require daily levers (concrete execution) and immediate first action
- Don't allow intellectual answers without emotional honesty or plans without first action
