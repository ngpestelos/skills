# Capability Building Roadmap

Transform abstract goals into concrete, executable 90-day action plans.

## Step 1: Assess Current Stage
For each domain, identify Dreyfus stage (Novice → Advanced Beginner → Competent → Proficient → Expert) with **specific evidence** — not self-perception.

## Step 2: Design Parallel Pillars
Capabilities should reinforce each other, not compete. Design parallel tracks where progress in one pillar accelerates the others.

## Step 3: Time-Calibrated Action Plan

**First question**: "What's your realistic weekly time budget?" Plans must fit reality, not aspiration.

```
Time Budget: X hours/week

Month 1: [Theme]
- Week 1-2: [Activities] (~X hrs each)
- Week 3-4: [Activities]

Month 2-3: [Continue...]
```

Don't accept aspirational timelines disconnected from available hours.
