# Active Reading to Wisdom

Transform reading into behavioral change. The goal is not knowledge accumulation but life application.

## Step 1: Pre-Reading Intention

Before opening any book or article:

```markdown
**Book**: [Title]
**Why This Book Now**: What drew me to this at this moment?
**Questions**: What do I hope to learn or resolve?
**Application Context**: What current challenges might this address?
```

## Step 2: Application Bridge

After reading (or after synthesis/progressive summary):

```markdown
**Insight**: [Key wisdom from reading]
**Situation**: [Specific current life context]
**Action**: [Concrete step to apply this wisdom]
**Timeline**: [When will I implement this?]
```

Test insights against real experience. Document results. Refine understanding based on practice.

**The measure of reading success is behavioral change, not highlights collected.**
