# Regret Minimization Framework

## The 10-10-10 Rule

| Horizon | Question | Reveals |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **10 Minutes** | How will I feel? | Emotional noise — discount it |
| **10 Months** | Practical consequences? | Transition difficulty vs. adaptation |
| **10 Years** | Will this matter? | Fundamental values and life direction |

## Two Types of Regret

| | Action | Inaction |
|---|---|---|
| **Over time** | Usually fades | Usually grows |
| **Learning** | Extracted from experience | None (nothing tried) |
| **Correctability** | Often correctable | Moment passed |

## Minimum Regret Path

Choose the path where even if it fails, you can live with the outcome and the story of having tried. At 80, you'll regret not trying more than trying and failing.

**Minimize, don't eliminate** — accept regret is unavoidable. Choose the regret you can live with. Check projection bias: future self adapts in ways current self can't imagine.
