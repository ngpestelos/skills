# Rumination Interrupt

Immediate interventions for breaking rumination patterns in 30 seconds or less, with pathways to deeper protocols.

## Core Insight

"You don't think your way out of rumination — you act your way out." Rumination creates an illusion of permanence through repetition. Physical action interrupts the mental loop *before* cognitive reframing can occur.

## 30-Second Interventions

### 1. Three-Part Physical Interrupt

| Action | Why It Works |
|--------|--------------|
| Stand up and walk (even 30 sec) | Physical movement interrupts neural loop |
| Change rooms or go outside | Environmental shift creates internal shift |
| Do one tiny physical task | Forward movement breaks stasis |

### 2. CIA "Next Fastest Thing" Method
> **"What is the next task I can complete in the shortest amount of time?"**

Bypasses compromised rational brain. Uses time as objective guide. Builds momentum through quick wins.

### 3. High Agency Escape Route
> **"How can I take action on this NOW?"**
> **Reframe: Decisions are experiments, not permanent choices.**

Clear thinking (define problem outside your head) → Bias to action (physical interrupts mental) → Disagreeability (reject paralysis as acceptable).

## Deeper Protocols

**DMN Reset (5 min)**: Breathing (4-4-6) → Physical anchoring → Cognitive reframing → Gratitude shift → Autosuggestion.

**Prayerful Pause**: When asked a question, pause and ask what you *actually* think rather than responding with cached positions.

Don't dismiss small actions — washing one dish IS the intervention. Unchecked rumination compounds over time — early intervention prevents entrenchment.
