# Cutting Identifier

## The Question

**"If I had only one hour per day for [domain], what would I do?"**

Write your answer:
`My cutting in [domain] is [specific outcome].`

## Is It Right?

| Yes | No |
|-----|-----|
| Decisions become obvious | Still torn between priorities |
| Energy increases when doing it | Procrastinating on "my cutting" |

## Maintenance

Review weekly. Revise when domain changes or after 3+ days of misalignment.

## Limits

Multiple cuttings may be necessary for parallel demands (parenting + career).
If singular focus produces anxiety, this framework may not fit.

## Troubleshooting

**Can't answer in 10 seconds?** The domain is too broad — subdivide it (e.g., "career" → "this quarter's deliverables").
