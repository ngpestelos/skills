# Agency Diagnostic

Diagnose what blocks action across three layers, then prescribe immediate action.

| Layer | Question |
|-------|----------|
| **Blindness** | Am I misattributing constraints to external factors? |
| **Traps** | Which behavioral pattern keeps me stuck? |
| **Voices** | What self-limiting voice is blocking action? |

## Layer 1: Blindness

Capture: "I can't [action] because [constraint]." Then apply:

| Pattern | Blind Spot | Reframe |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Permission** | "I need permission to..." | "What if I just do it?" |
| **Qualification** | "I'm not qualified to..." | "What would I do if I were?" |
| **Timing** | "Not the right time..." | "Smallest step I could take now?" |
| **Resource** | "Don't have resources..." | "What could I do with what I have?" |
| **Network** | "Don't know the right people..." | "What if I reached out directly?" |

## Layer 2: Low-Agency Traps

| Trap | Indicator | Escape |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Vague** | "I don't know what I want" | "What does your dream week look like hour by hour?" |
| **Attachment** | "It has to be this way" | "If this was off the table, what else?" |
| **Midwit** | "It's more nuanced than that" | "Simplest version that could work?" |
| **Rumination** | "But what if..." | "Smallest action in the next 5 minutes?" |
| **Social Norms** | "That's just how it's done" | "If no one would ever know, what would you do?" |

**When multiple traps present, start with Vague** — it's usually the root.

## Layer 3: Internal Voices

| Voice | Message | Disguise | Antidote |
|-------|---------|----------|----------|
| **Nihilistic** | "Nothing matters" | Philosophical doubt, cynicism | Action producing visible result |
| **Inadequacy** | "You aren't good enough" | False humility, perfectionism | Action small enough to not trigger fear |
| **Identity** | "You're a loser" | "Self-awareness", history-based reasoning | Action contradicting the identity story |

## Always end with:

**"What ONE thing can you do in the next 5 minutes?"**
